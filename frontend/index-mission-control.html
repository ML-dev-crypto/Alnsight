FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why AInSight? +
AInSight brings enterprise-grade AI capabilities to your organization without the complexity, cost, or privacy concerns of traditional AI platforms. Run powerful language models directly in the browser with zero API costs and complete data privacy.
What AI models are supported? +
We support multiple state-of-the-art models including TinyLlama (1.1B), RedPajama (3B), Phi-3 Mini (4B), Mistral 7B, and Llama 3 8B. All models run locally in your browser using WebGPU acceleration for fast inference.
How does browser-based AI work? +
We use WebLLM and WebGPU technology to run AI models directly in your browser. The model downloads once, caches locally, and then runs entirely on your device using GPU acceleration. No data ever leaves your machine.
What are the system requirements? +
You need a modern browser with WebGPU support (Chrome 113+, Edge 113+, or equivalent), a decent GPU (integrated graphics work for smaller models), and enough storage for the AI model (600MB - 6GB depending on model choice).
Is AInSight suitable for my business? +
AInSight is perfect for businesses that need AI capabilities but are concerned about data privacy, API costs, or internet connectivity. It's ideal for healthcare, legal, finance, field operations, and any organization handling sensitive data.
